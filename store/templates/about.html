About Us

Forevergreen cacti is an online store for those looking to grow or start their cacti collection. All cacti and succulents found on this store have been grown from seed with love.
By logging into Forevergreen Cacti you can take advantage of user benefits such as creating a User Profile, view your Shopping Cart, and view your purchasing history!
Forevergreen cacti products are ideal for gifts, bringing your interior and exterior design ideas to the next level and can add a unique element to your home. Another benefit to having cacti in your home is that all forms of cacti are considered to be among the best plants at reducing the levels of bacteria and radiation in their surroundings. Carbon dioxide is absorbed by the cacti and succulents and converted to oxygen. This is why we strive to grow and propagate only high quality cacti and succulents.
